# Nuro Help & Support

---

## Welcome to Nuro

Nuro is the AI for Tech Operations platform that helps hosting operations teams investigate infrastructure and service health using natural language, cross-domain insights and guided recommendations.

Nuro currently supports and is expanding across key operational domains including network, identity, cloud/edge, backup, observability and Kubernetes/container insights, with a roadmap towards proactive detection, remediation, predictive analysis, simulation and self-healing.

---

## Quick Start

1. Sign in using your GSK credentials.
2. For access, apply for one of the following EntraId groups:
   - `GSK-Nuro-Hosting-Dev-User-csg`
   - `GSK-Nuro-Conn-Dev-User-csg`
   - `GSK-Nuro-IAM-Dev-User-csg`
3. Check which skills are available in the platform:
   - `Show available skills`
   - `Show tools`
4. Ask clear, outcome-focused questions in natural language.
5. Share feedback to help improve capability quality and user experience.
